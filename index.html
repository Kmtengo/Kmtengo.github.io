About Us


What is MksU Hackfest?

MksU Hackfest 2023 is a campus level hackathon organized to nurture creativity, foster relentless innovation, and empower talented minds to tackle real-world challenges. Presented by the Machakos University Tech Community, this marks the inaugural edition of "MksU HackFest." Rooted in a commitment to nurturing problem-solving, fostering collaboration, and sparking creativity, "MksU Hackfest 2023" stands as a powerful catalyst propelling our nation into the forefront of pioneering innovation and transformative technologies. We wholeheartedly invite you to embark on this exhilarating journey of exploration and creation alongside us.


Our Mission

The Mission of MksU Hackfest is to nurture boundless creativity, foster relentless innovation, and empower talented minds to tackle real-world challenges head-on. We believe in the transformative power of technology and the potential of every individual to make a difference. In a testament to the power of collaboration, we've joined hands with Machakos University Innovation Club, Microsoft Learn Students Ambassador – Machakos University Chapter, Google Developer Student Club – Machakos University Chapter and Techo-Engineering Association of Machakos University and together, we are creating an extraordinary event that transcends faculties and disciplines, bringing programmers, developers, designers, engineers, and tech enthusiasts under one roof.


Why Participate?

Participating in MksU Hackfest 2023 offers a platform to learn and apply new skills, collaborate within a team, and solve real-world problems under time constraints. It presents opportunities to network with like-minded individuals, gain exposure to emerging technologies, and potentially earn recognition and rewards.




CATEGORIES

Energy & Infrastructure

Participants are encouraged to tackle challenges related to energy production, conservation, and sustainability.

This can include projects focused on renewable energy sources, energy efficiency solutions, smart grids, or innovative ways to reduce energy consumption.

Water

This category is centered around addressing water-related issues, such as water scarcity, pollution, conservation, and access to clean water.

Participants can develop solutions to monitor water quality, improve water management, or promote water conservation practices.

Agriculture

This category focuses on innovations in agriculture and food production.

Participants can work on projects related to precision farming, crop management, sustainable agriculture practices, or technologies to improve food supply chains.

Software Engineering

The Software category is a versatile category that encourages participants to develop software solutions to a wide range of problems.

This includes Artificial Intelligence solutions, mobile apps, web applications, data analysis tools, or software platforms that address specific challenges in any domain.

Out of the Box

The Out of the Box category is for the most creative and unconventional projects that don't fit neatly into the other categories.

It's an opportunity for participants to explore unique, imaginative, and groundbreaking ideas that can have a real-world impact.

It’s absolutely free. Well, you do need to invest your time and enthusiasm though.

Beforehand, connect with us on social media to meet potential teammates. You can also invite your friends to join the hackathon, so you might see some familiar faces there.
